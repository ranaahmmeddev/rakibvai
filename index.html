My Core Services

Comprehensive photo editing solutions tailored to elevate your brand's visual identity and drive sales.

Clipping Path

Precise, hand-drawn clipping paths for perfect background removal, ensuring clean edges for any product type.

Photo Retouching

High-end skin, product, and dust retouching to make your subject look flawless while keeping natural textures.

Ghost Mannequin

Professional neck joint and invisible mannequin effects for apparel, giving garments a 3D hollow look.

Color Correction

Accurate color matching, exposure fixing, and white balance adjustment to ensure product colors are true to life.

Shadow Creation

Adding natural drop, reflection, or cast shadows to give products depth and a realistic, grounded appearance.

Image Masking

Advanced masking for complex objects like hair, fur, or transparent items where standard clipping paths fail.
